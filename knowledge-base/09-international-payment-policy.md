# International Payment Policy
**Document ID:** POL-INTL-001  
**Last Updated:** January 18, 2025  
**Owner:** International Expansion Team  
**Status:** Active

## Overview
This policy outlines GothPay's current international payment capabilities, restrictions, and future expansion plans. As of January 2025, GothPay operates ONLY within the United States and select U.S. territories.

---

## Supported Regions

### ✓ Fully Supported

**United States:**
- All 50 states
- Full functionality (send, receive, instant transfers, all features)

**U.S. Territories:**
- Puerto Rico
- U.S. Virgin Islands
- Guam
- American Samoa (limited - see special notes)
- Northern Mariana Islands (limited - see special notes)

### ✗ Not Supported

**International (Any country outside U.S.):**
- Cannot send money to international recipients
- Cannot receive money from international senders
- Cannot use GothPay while physically located outside U.S. (see Travel section)

**Other Territories:**
- Military bases abroad: Supported (see Military section)
- U.S. embassies: Not supported

## Geographic Restrictions Explained

### Why GothPay is U.S.-Only

**Regulatory Compliance:**
- Each country has different money transmission laws
- Anti-money laundering (AML) requirements vary by jurisdiction
- Know Your Customer (KYC) standards differ internationally
- Currency exchange regulations

**Banking Partnerships:**
- GothPay's partner banks operate primarily in U.S.
- Cross-border payments require different banking infrastructure
- SWIFT/international wire systems not integrated

**Business Decision:**
- Focus on perfecting U.S. experience first
- International expansion planned (see Future Plans section)

### What Counts as "International"

**These are considered international (NOT supported):**
- Sending money to someone with foreign address
- Receiving money from someone outside U.S.
- Using GothPay app while traveling outside U.S.
- Using VPN to appear in U.S. while actually abroad (violates Terms of Service)

**These are considered domestic (SUPPORTED):**
- U.S. citizen living in U.S. sending to another U.S.-based person
- U.S. account sending to U.S. territory account
- Active military stationed abroad using APO/FPO address (see Military section)

## Traveling Internationally

### Limited Functionality While Abroad

**What You CAN Do:**
- Check account balance
- View transaction history
- Contact customer support
- Receive money (to your U.S.-based account)
- Download statements

**What You CANNOT Do:**
- Send money to anyone
- Add new payment methods (cards/bank accounts)
- Verify identity (location verification required)
- Withdraw funds
- Make instant transfers

### Why the Restrictions?

**Fraud Prevention:**
- Unusual login locations trigger security flags
- Prevents account takeover from foreign IP addresses
- Reduces risk of money laundering through location spoofing

**Compliance:**
- Some countries prohibit foreign payment apps
- GothPay not licensed to operate in most foreign jurisdictions
- Legal liability concerns

### Restoring Full Access

**Return to U.S.:**
- Full functionality automatically restored when you're back in U.S.
- May need to verify location (GPS, IP address check)
- First transaction after return may require additional verification

**Extended International Stay:**
If you'll be abroad for extended period:
- Consider withdrawing funds before departure
- Set up auto-transfers to U.S. bank account
- Inform GothPay support of travel dates (optional, helps prevent fraud alerts)

### VPN Usage

**Not Permitted While Abroad:**
- Using VPN to appear in U.S. while actually abroad violates Terms of Service
- If detected, may result in:
  - Account suspension
  - Transaction holds
  - Mandatory re-verification
  - Potential account closure

**Permitted in U.S.:**
- VPN use while physically in U.S. is allowed
- May trigger additional security verifications
- Some payment restrictions may apply until location confirmed

## U.S. Territories - Special Notes

### Puerto Rico
- **Status:** Fully supported, same as U.S. states
- **Currency:** U.S. Dollar (USD)
- **Banking:** U.S. banking regulations apply
- **Verification:** Same requirements as mainland U.S.

### U.S. Virgin Islands
- **Status:** Fully supported
- **Currency:** U.S. Dollar (USD)
- **Notes:** Some banks may take longer to process (add 1-2 days to standard timelines)

### Guam
- **Status:** Fully supported
- **Time zone:** Transfers may appear delayed due to time difference
- **Banking:** Standard U.S. banking but international processing may add time

### American Samoa
- **Status:** Limited support
- **Issues:** Some banks not integrated; verify bank is supported before linking
- **Workaround:** May need to use mainland U.S. bank account

### Northern Mariana Islands
- **Status:** Limited support
- **Issues:** Similar to American Samoa
- **Recommendation:** Contact support before creating account to verify compatibility

## Military & Government Personnel

### Active Duty Military

**APO/FPO/DPO Addresses:**
- **Fully supported:** Treated as domestic U.S. addresses
- Can use GothPay while stationed abroad if using military network
- Must provide APO/FPO address as registered address

**Verification:**
- Military ID accepted as government-issued ID
- May need to provide orders showing current duty station
- Enhanced verification may be required

**Deployment Considerations:**
If deploying to combat zone or limited internet access:
- Set up trusted devices before deployment
- Enable 2FA with backup codes (save securely)
- Consider designating emergency contact
- Withdraw funds or set up auto-transfers if needed

### Government Employees Abroad

**Embassy/Consulate Staff:**
- Generally not supported (embassy addresses are foreign)
- May be able to maintain account with U.S. home address
- Limited functionality while at embassy

**State Department/Foreign Service:**
- Contact support for case-by-case evaluation
- May qualify for military-style exception with documentation

## Alternative Solutions for International Payments

Since GothPay doesn't support international transfers, users should consider:

### Wire Transfers
- **Best for:** Large one-time payments
- **Pros:** Secure, direct bank-to-bank
- **Cons:** Expensive ($25-$50 per transfer), slow (3-7 days)
- **How:** Through your bank

### TransferWise (Wise)
- **Best for:** Regular international payments, currency exchange
- **Pros:** Low fees, good exchange rates, fast (1-3 days)
- **Cons:** Verification required, limits on some countries
- **How:** wise.com

### PayPal (International)
- **Best for:** Small payments, online purchases
- **Pros:** Widely accepted, integrated with many platforms
- **Cons:** High fees (4-5%), poor exchange rates
- **How:** paypal.com

### Western Union / MoneyGram
- **Best for:** Cash pickup, emergency transfers, countries with limited banking
- **Pros:** Fast, cash pickup option, widely available
- **Cons:** Very high fees, lower limits
- **How:** In-person or online

### Cryptocurrency
- **Best for:** Tech-savvy users, peer-to-peer transfers
- **Pros:** Fast, low fees, no geographic restrictions
- **Cons:** Volatile, complex, regulatory uncertainty, irreversible
- **How:** Coinbase, Kraken, etc. (Note: GothPay does not currently support cryptocurrency - see separate crypto policy if implemented)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I'm a U.S. citizen living abroad permanently. Can I use GothPay?**
A: Not reliably. You need U.S. address, U.S. phone number, and U.S. location to send money. Receiving may work but sending will be blocked.

**Q: Can I send money from my U.S. account to my friend's U.S. account while I'm on vacation in Mexico?**
A: No. Sending is blocked when you're physically located outside the U.S., even if both accounts are U.S.-based.

**Q: What if I'm on a cruise ship in international waters?**
A: May work depending on ship's internet/satellite location detection. Not officially supported.

**Q: My bank account is with a U.S. bank but I live abroad. Can I use GothPay?**
A: No. Your physical location must be in U.S. to send payments.

**Q: Can I receive money from someone in another country if I have a U.S. account?**
A: Only if they also have a U.S.-based GothPay account and are physically in the U.S. when sending.

**Q: Will GothPay notify me when international support is available?**
A: Yes. We'll send email and in-app notifications when new regions launch.

**Q: Can I pre-register for international beta?**
A: Not currently. Follow our blog (blog.gothpay.com) and social media for announcements.

## Compliance & Legal

### Sanctions & Embargoes

GothPay complies with all U.S. sanctions and embargoes:
- Cannot process payments to/from sanctioned countries
- Accounts of sanctioned individuals will be frozen
- OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) compliance required

**Currently Sanctioned Countries Include:**
- Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Russia (partial), others
- List subject to change based on U.S. government policy

### Know Your Customer (KYC) - International

If/when international support launches:
- Separate identity verification for non-U.S. accounts
- Country-specific ID requirements
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk countries
- Different verification levels and limits
